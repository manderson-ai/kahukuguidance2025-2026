Step-by-Step AVID Reading Strategies

  1. Analyze the Title: Predict what the article is about based on the title.
  2. Number the Paragraphs: Write small numbers next to each paragraph to refer to them easily.
  3. Circle the Main Idea: Find and circle the sentence that tells the main idea of each paragraph.
  4. Underline the Author‚Äôs Claims: Underline the sentences where the author makes key statements or arguments.
  5. Highlight Unfamiliar Vocabulary: Highlight any words you do not know and infer their meaning from context.
  6. Summarize Each Paragraph: In the margin, write a 1‚Äì2 word summary of what each paragraph is about.
  7. Ask One Question Per Paragraph: Write a question in the margin that arises from each paragraph‚Äôs content.
  8. Make a Text-to-Self Connection: Write one sentence about how the information relates to your own life.
  9. Write a One-Sentence Summary: At the bottom of the article, write one sentence that captures the gist of the entire text.
